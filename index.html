How You Can Support My Work

There are several ways — most of them free — to support me and my work.

You might be thinking:
“But I already pay for your courses. Isn't that enough?”

And yes — that support means the world to me. But one day, you — like many others I’ve appreciated and cared for — may move on. And when that happens, who will help those still learning?

SmarterGerman thrives through a constant flow of new learners. You may not think about that much, but here’s why it matters:

Accessibility matters:
My courses are already affordable. If you're on a tight budget, you can apply for a “student discount” — no student ID needed — and pay only €9.90/mo. Still too much? There's also a €1/mo scholarship, in return for a short monthly progress report — just to stay accountable.

I love improving tools, refining your experience, and building powerful new exercises. But creativity needs time — and time needs funding. If I’m worried about money, I can’t focus fully on giving you the best learning journey.

Even the full plan at €29.90/mo is a bargain compared to any serious competitor — and no, I’m not talking about apps with talking owls.

I care about your progress.
I know what it’s like to feel stuck — to not want to study, especially German. I'm empathetic (though being from Northern Germany, also quite direct). I truly enjoy hearing about your wins, struggles, and setbacks — they help me make this course even better.

Your support helps thousands of learners — not just me, not just you.
Thank you for being part of this journey.
